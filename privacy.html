1. Information We Handle

When you connect a third-party platform (such as TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram), the Service stores the access and refresh tokens needed to publish content to your own account. These are stored locally on the operator's own machine and are used only to perform actions you request.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You may revoke the Service's access to any connected platform at any time through that platform's settings, which invalidates the stored tokens. You may also delete locally stored data at any time. To request assistance, contact us using the details below.
